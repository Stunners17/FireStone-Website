MORE ABOUT US

We are a Family-owned business founded in 2010 by Grace Mnguni. Our mission is to provide high-quality coal products while prioritizing sustainability and customer satisfaction. Over the years, we have expanded our operations and built a reputation for reliability and excellence in the coal industry.

For over a decade,we have been supplying reliable coal for cooking,heating, and everyday household use. Our focus hasalways been simple: supplying coal that burns well with fair prices while ensuring environmental responsibility. We source our coal from trusted mines that adhere to strict environmental standards, minimizing our ecological footprint. As a family business, we understand the importance of community and trust and that's why we continue to serve our community with the same values we started with honesty and respect.

Today we are proud to continue the legacy, supplying coal to local households and businesses, and remaining rooted in the community that supported us from the beginning.
